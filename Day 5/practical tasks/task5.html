Deep Sea Exploration

Published on November 21, 2025

The deep sea remains one of the least explored areas on Earth. Scientists continue to discover unique species and ecosystems that exist under extreme pressure and darkness. Advancements in underwater robotics and submersibles are helping us uncover these mysteries.
